Where Does Your Waste Go?

Here's how your segregated waste gets responsibly handled across Delhi NCR.

Plastics / Glass / Paper
(Dry Waste)

  1. JAAGRUTI / We-Recycle — Waste-paper recycling service across 400+ institutions in Delhi NCR.
  2. Econscious — Social startup doing post-consumer plastic collection & recycling in Delhi NCR.
  3. WMARS — Works on plastic, paper, and dry waste recycling in NCR.
  4. IPCA — Runs plastic / dry-waste management and MRFs in NCR.

Food / Organic Waste

  1. EcoPolluTech — Makes composting machines in Delhi NCR to convert food waste into compost.
  2. Saahas (Zero Waste Program) — Works on food-waste composting and integrated zero-waste systems for communities.
  3. Rise Foundation — Local NGO in Delhi NCR running community composting and food-waste management.
  4. Delhi Parks & Gardens Society (DPGS) — Runs authorized compost pits in Delhi for wet-waste / leaf / organic collection.
